Who are we?

JCI Amsterdam International is a personal development organisation for young professionals and entrepreneurs. We are part of a global network of more than 200,000 young and active people in their twenties and thirties, which is present in more than 100 countries.

By joining our chapter, you are automatically a member of JCI the Netherlands. Becoming a JCI member means being part of a global network of like globally minded citizens who believe in improving their local communities through active citizenship.

Innovative solutions

What do we do?

There are limits to what the government is able to achieve. In that sense, we take action to help improve the Amsterdam community through development initiatives.

We believe that positive change starts with the individual social responsibility (ISR). We think critically about the greatest challenges faced by our community, Amsterdam, and take action to be a part of the solution.

Sustainable solutions

Why?

Believe or not, being a globally minded individual means having rights and responsibilities, as well as shared goals. By joining our chapter, you will live out your sense of social responsibility and be part of an organisation that seeks to improve communities for future generations.

People join JCI chapters for many different reasons. They might have a problem that they want to fix or an obstacle that they want to overcome. No matter why you join, you will always be in the presence of like-minded individuals who will share your vision for a better future.
