The Big Idea

Imagine what a community could become if young people from different backgrounds work together to find targeted solutions to local issues and address the critical challenges of our time while developing the skills, knowledge and understanding. We're not only talking about charity or service, but also about sustainable impact locally and globally.

We are JCI Amsterdam International JCI, the first English-speaking chapter in the Netherlands. We are a team of young, enthusiastic, globally-minded people who work or live in the Amsterdam area. Our members come from everywhere, work in diverse fields and different backgrounds. We use our time within the chapter to promote trade, commerce, community and culture, while developing our professional and social skills.
